About

Built For WASAC And Every Citizen

AquaTrack Rwanda is a smart water management system designed to reduce non-revenue water by combining monitoring, analytics and citizen reporting into one modern platform.

For WASAC

Monitor water infrastructure, receive real-time alerts, analyze NRW trends, assign field teams and manage reports from a single dashboard.

For Citizens

Report leaks, faulty meters and illegal connections quickly while tracking report progress through an easy-to-use platform.

For Rwanda

Support Rwanda's Vision 2050 by improving water management, reducing losses and increasing service transparency.
